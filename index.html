Chocolate Smoothie

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Serving Size: 2

  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 banana
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  1. Place all ingredients into a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  3. Pour into glasses and enjoy!
